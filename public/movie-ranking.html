The Best Movies According to Olivia

My top 3 movies of all time.

Legally Blonde

Elle Woods is very ND coded and I love the way she knows what she wants and goes for it.

Finding Nemo

A moving story about how the love of family, however unconventional, can overcome any challenge.

Forrest Gump

A reminder that sometimes naivety and simplicity are the best way to get where you're supposed to be.
